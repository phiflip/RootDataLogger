LoadCell Wiring Guide

### LoadCell Connection LoadCell to HX711

Connector Pin | HX711 Connection
--------------|------------------
1             | E+
2             | E-
3             | A-
4             | A+
5             | GND (braid)

### Additional Notes for 6-Wire LoadCell Setup
For 6-wire LoadCells, the Sense wires are connected to:
- Sense+ → E+
- Sense- → E-
